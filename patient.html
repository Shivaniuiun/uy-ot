Your First Unique Visit

Your first visit is straightforward, pain and pressure-free, relaxed & informative. Dr. Schmidt will complete a comprehensive orthodontic examination, take necessary x-rays, and scan your teeth with our iTero digital scanner. From that assessment, he will determine and review your individual orthodontic needs and create a customized treatment plan just for you. Our team will also discuss the cost, treatment duration, and what to expect during your treatment to help you make an informed decision for yourself or your child.
