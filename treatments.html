Customized Orthodontics. Exceptional Service.

Braces

At Uniquely You Orthodontics, we utilize modern technology to provide our patients with a more efficient and comfortable orthodontic experience. We use the Damon Bracket System, which doesn't use colored elastic ties to keep the wire in place. The Damon brackets are smaller and low profile, forgiving to surrounding tissues. With its sliding door mechanism, it keeps the wire in place while reducing friction and making treatment faster, more efficient, and more comfortable.

To shorten your braces bonding appointment, we use digital AI software to create 3D-printed trays, guiding us on where to bond each bracket accurately. We can also show a simulation of your teeth' final positions before bonding your braces, improving communication and patient expectations.

Technology

iTero

Say goodbye to the goop, gagging, and extreme discomfort you experienced with dental rubber impressions. At Uniquely You Orthodontics, we use iTero Digital Scanners to accurately take a picture of your teeth and create a 3D digital image comfortably with our "digital wands." It can capture the nooks and crannies of your teeth to make a highly accurate 3D digital model, allowing us to create a more fitting and precise orthodontic appliance.

CBCT

You don't have to leave our office to get a CBCT (Cone Beam Computed Tomography Scanner)! You can have your 3D CBCT taken in our office to help us better understand your teeth, jaw bones, and other surrounding structures that may affect your treatment. The radiation dose may be up to 100 times lower than that of a typical computed tomography scanner and aids in providing you with a more comprehensive and personalized treatment. .
